About TLF BBQ

We’re a family‑owned BBQ and catering company based in Morristown, NJ, serving slow‑smoked meats and bold flavors since 2018.

Our team is led by Chef Bryan Gregg, formerly of Think Local Foods and Escape Montclair, who brings restaurant‑quality technique to every plate.

We focus on local ingredients, seasonal menus, and consistent hospitality that keeps our guests coming back.

Our Mission

To bring high‑quality BBQ and catering to Morristown and the surrounding communities, with a focus on flavor, freshness, and friendly service.

Meet The Chef

Chef Bryan Gregg is an award‑winning chef celebrated for his refined technique, respect for ingredients, and dedication to New Jersey’s local producers.

He founded Think Local Foods, led acclaimed restaurants such as Escape Montclair, and launched Tastes by Bryan Gregg.
